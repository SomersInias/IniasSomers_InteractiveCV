My journey into AI began during my Bachelor in Electronics-ICT — a single inspiring talk from someone in the field ignited a passion that's never stopped growing.

I completed an internship at BrainJar (Raccoons), traveling daily to Heverlee-Leuven for six months to work on NLP techniques. The experience confirmed what I knew: AI is where I belong.

Since then I've independently explored ComfyUI, RAG pipelines, Stable Diffusion, StyleGAN2-ADA, LangChain, LangGraph and more — building real projects, not just following tutorials.

My bachelor was completed with distinction, without a single resit — but what drives me isn't grades. It's the genuine need to understand deeply and build things that matter.
